Phuket

Patong Beach

Known for its vibrant nightlife, bustling beach scene, and myriad of restaurants and bars.

Highlights: Bangla Road, Patong Beach, Jungceylon Shopping Center.

Phi Phi Islands

A stunning island group known for crystal-clear waters, picturesque beaches, and excellent diving spots.

Highlights: Maya Bay, Viking Cave, Koh Phi Phi Leh.

Old Phuket Town

Rich in culture and history, featuring Sino-Portuguese architecture, street art, and local markets.

Highlights: Thalang Road, Phuket Sunday Walking Street Market, Baan Chinpracha.
